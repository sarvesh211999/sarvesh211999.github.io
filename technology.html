Machine Learning Models

Ultra Large Virtual Screening

Our proprietary algorithm MEMES leverages Bayesian methods to screen libraries of 100s of millions of molecules 20x faster to quickly get you the best hits.

Low cost, exceptionally accurate property prediction

Our multi-property deep-learning model DeepDrugProfile accurately predicts drug properties across physical chemistry, absorption, metabolism, excretion and toxicity, at a very low computational cost. Our pipeline leverages few-shot learning methods to provide accurate predictions to your project-specific tasks, even if the data availability is low or expensive.

Large Scale Chemical Space Exploration

Break the limits of exploration with GoogolGenPlus, which leverages Deep Reinforcement learning to explore a Chemical space billion times larger than traditional methods and get the best hits. Best part ? It does so while optimizing tens of properties across ADME, toxicity, affinity, all while ensuring synthesizability.

Multi-Parameter Molecule Optimization

DeepMolOpt helps you optimize your leads w.r.t tens of important drug properties across ADME, toxicity, affinity, all while ensuring synthesizability.

Synthetic Route Optimizer

DeepRetro will help your medicinal chemist to identify synthetic routes to most promising compounds.
