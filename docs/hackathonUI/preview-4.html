A great choice for everyday PC usage. The slim, sleek Acer K2 Series K202HQL monitor provides exceptional image quality, superior visual comfort, and other essential features – such as multiple connection options, adjustable mechanism etc., all at a price point that'll make you smile. You'll enjoy tasks and entertainment more on the 19.5” LED-backlit display. It presents incredibly clear, rich-detailed images at a high resolution of 1600 x 900, and features an impressive dynamic contrast ratio of 100,000,000:1 to reveal darker image areas in greater depth. A super-fast 5-millisecond response time displays action sequences with the highest degree of clarity. The great sights are made even better by exceptional colors via Acer Adaptive Contrast Management. The ComfyView display prevents reflections to provide greater eye comfort. It also delivers brighter colors for you viewing enjoyment. Set the display angle to optimize your view with its ergonomic stand that tilts from -5 to 25 degrees. The included VGA and DVI interfaces provide flexibility in connectivity. So whatever devices your employees need to connect today or in the future, this monitor keeps everyone’s options open. Safe for you and your world, K2 Series monitors feature mercury-free white LED backlighting and meet ENERGY STAR 6.0 requirements. Acer EcoDisplay technologies use arsenic-free display glass, further protecting the environment. Acer packaging is also made of recyclable materials.
